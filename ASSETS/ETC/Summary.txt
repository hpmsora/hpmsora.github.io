Won Yong Ha is a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computing Engineering at NYU-Poly, graduating in 2024. He holds a Master's in Systems Engineering from Cornell and a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Indiana University Bloomington. His expertise includes programming, optimization, simulation, and embedded systems.</br></br>


With over two years as a Military Science Researcher, Won Yong Ha created a storage optimization webpage using Java, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript, leading to a publication in the Korea Military Science Journal. At NYU, he contributed to projects in transit routing and autonomous driving, developing systems for optical routing and Lidar sensor software. Passionate about applying his skills to solve real-world problems, he seeks to collaborate with diverse teams to advance science and technology.

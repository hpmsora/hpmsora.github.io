Won Yong Ha is a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computing Engineering at New York University - Polytechnic School of Engineering, with an expected graduation in 2024. He holds a Master of Engineering in Systems Engineering from Cornell University and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Indiana University Bloomington. His core competencies include programming, optimization, simulation, and embedded systems.</br></br>

With over two years of experience as a Military Science Researcher at the Korea Army Consolidated Supply Depot, Won Yong Ha has demonstrated his expertise in research and development. He created a storage optimization webpage utilizing Java, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript, and employed a genetic algorithm to optimize warehouse storage, which led to a publication in the Korea Military Science Journal. Additionally, his tenure as a Research Assistant at Cornell University College of Engineering has been marked by significant contributions to projects related to transit routing and autonomous driving. He developed a system for optical routing in small-area transit and a software for the Lidar sensor, as well as a Lidar real-time communication program for multiple devices such as Raspberry Pi and MCUs.</br></br>

Passionate about applying his skills and knowledge to solve real-world problems and create innovative solutions, Won Yong Ha is eager to learn from and collaborate with diverse and talented teams, contributing significantly to the advancement of science and technology.
